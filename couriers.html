Are you friendly, passionate and knowledgeable about cannabis, and want to help Canary kickstart the cannabis technology industry? Join our team as a Canary Courier and be a part of our vision to revolutionize the cannabis technology and infrastructure world.

Who You Are

Our Couriers are the face of the Canary brand, and we're looking for great people to fill those shoes.

Knowledgeable

You have experience with cannabis, either as a user, patient, or advocate. It's best that you're well versed in different product types and strain attributes. You don't need to be an expert, but should have general knowledge about cannabis.

Friendly

You're outgoing and friendly, entrepreneurial, and always happy to explain the ins-and-outs of marijuana to new users. Couriers are the only point of human interaction that Canary customers have with the brand - be ready to represent the brand.

Local

You live in one of our expected launch cities – Seattle, Denver, LA, or San Francisco. But even if you don't, put your name down anyway – our vision is for a national Canary launch as soon as the government gets on board with legalization.
