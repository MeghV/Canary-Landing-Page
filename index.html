How It Works

You're only 3 steps away from delivery (after we know you're legit).

Verification

Canary abides by all recreational and medicinal cannabis on a state-by-state basis. When you first sign on, we'll need proof that you're able to enjoy cannabis legally in your state - you'll take a picture of your ID or medicinal marijuana card during signup and send it to us for verification in-app. Once verified, you're good to order.

Strains

We believe that you should be able to explore and enjoy all strains of cannabis, not just the strains available at your local dispensary. We're partnering with dispensaries and producers to connect you with strains you love and strains you will love. You'll be able to order quantities in grams to ounces, depending on your state's cannabis laws.

Pay

Just like Uber, Postmates, Lyft, and your other favorite delivery services, payment occurs through your phone.

Delivered

Once you've ordered, we'll send our background-checked and legally employed couriers to deliver your cannabis to your chosen location within our delivery zones.
